[PARTNERING PACKAGES] Berkeley Innovation (BI) is UC Berkeley's premier student-run, human-centered design consultancy. Established in 2003, our mission is to be the center of human-centered design through education, practice, and thought leadership. We're a collection of thinkers, makers, tinkerers, and doers from a wide range of disciplines but with a shared love for design innovation.

To foster an interdisciplinary community of design thinkers and innovators and to encourage the application of human-centered design skills to real world problems.
