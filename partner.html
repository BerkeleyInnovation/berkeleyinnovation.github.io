Partner With Us

Semester-Long Projects

Each semester, BI works on ten-week long consulting projects with outside companies, as well as the university. If you are interested in proposing a project to us, feel free to reach out. We can help you with:

  • The Semester Project: Product Strategy Recommendations, UX Evaluations, User Research
  • Digital Renderings: UI/UX Component Libraries
  • Mock of Interaction & Visual: Invision/Marvel Prototypes, Detailed Hi-Fidelity User Flows, Scale Model
  • Additional Events and Recruit-based Benefits

Sponsorship

If you are unable to participate in our project program but would still like to donate, please considering being a sponsor. Some of the great benefits of being a sponsor include:

  • Exclusive Designathon Sponsor
  • Apparel + Marketing Recognition
  • Campus Infosession and Career Fairs
  • Additional Events and Recruit-based Benefits
