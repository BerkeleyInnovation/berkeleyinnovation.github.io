design with us

Established in 2003, Berkeley Innovation is UC Berkeley's Human Centered Design Consultancy. We unite makers, thinkers, tinkerers, and doers from a wide range of disciplines behind a shared love for design innovation, UI/UX, and interaction design. By joining us, you will be able to gain experience working with clients on design projects, network with student and professional designers, access design resoures, and join our community for guidance and feedback.
