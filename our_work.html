TIMELINE
WEEKS 1-2
USER RESEARCH
Determine the target user persona, create research guide, conduct research.
WEEKS 3-4
IDEATION & LO-FI
Synthesize research, create user experience storyboard, ideate lo-fi design solutions.
WEEKS 5-6
MID-FI & USER TESTING
Iterate on design solutions, create mid-fi wireframes, usability testing.
WEEKS 7-8
FINAL DELIVERABLES
Prepare final deliverables, create hi-fi prototype, final usability testing.
