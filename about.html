UC Berkeley's

Human-Centered

Design Consultancy

Berkeley Innovation (BI) is the home of human-centered design at UC Berkeley. Since 2003, we’ve united makers, thinkers, tinkerers, and doers from a wide range of disciplines behind a shared love for design innovation.

Our mission is to connect Berkeley students to collaborative innovation through education, practice, and thought leadership. Each semester, we provide educational opportunities through guest speakers, designathons, and design "bootcamps." Our consultants tackle real world design problems for our clients and industry partners. In addition to consultant work, we host a variety and social and career events to foster a strong design community at Cal.

Join Us

Do you ...

  • Love solving real world problems through human-centered design?
  • Seek more project experience in UI/UX?
  • Want to join a diverse community of design thinkers and innovators?

Then you belong at Berkeley Innovation! Over the first few weeks of every semester, BI recruits new designers to join the family. We welcome innovators from all disciplines, so don't let your unique background hinder your decision to apply. Make sure to follow our Facebook page for more detailed updates!
