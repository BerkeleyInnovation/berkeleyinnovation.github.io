SERVICES

At the beginning of the project, our consultants and leadership team will work to develop a set of clear and actionable deliverables with the client, based on the strategic and design needs. Throughout the design process, our consultants will work closely with clients during weekly meetings, where they will present progress and receive client feedback.

Depending on the nature of a client’s project, final deliverables may include:
• Product strategy recommendations
• User research reports
• User interface component library
• High fidelity mockups
• Interactive InVision prototype
